Opportunity Scoring

Every property gets a 0-100 score based on real data signals
  • +30 Long-term owner (15+ years)
  • +25 High equity position (50%+)
  • +15 Listed below assessed value
  • +10 Pre-1970 construction
  • +10 Multi-family property
  • +5 High walk score (80+)

Score 60+ = Hot Lead

Long-term owners sitting on massive equity. Highest-probability sellers. Direct mail + phone call.

Score 30-59 = Warm Lead

Good signals. Worth a direct mail touchpoint or cold email campaign.

Score <30 = Monitor

Keep in database. Revisit as market shifts.

SAY: "The scoring isn't random — it's based on real signals. A homeowner who's been there 20 years with 80% equity and a pre-1970 home? That's a 65+ score. They have a reason to sell, the equity to make it worth it, and the property probably needs updates. Compare that to someone who bought 2 years ago — they're a 10. You focus your time on the 60+ scores and let the system nurture the rest."

Every lead is checked against the National Do-Not-Call Registry before phone outreach. Flagged numbers are excluded automatically.
